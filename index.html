Global-budget.net

A Family Budget site for Expats and People who work across borders

When you live and work across borders your budget needs get more complex. If you are an Expat, Diplomat, or just someone who works with more than one currency this is the budget site for you.

Family Budgets are hard at the best of times, when you are dealing with several currencies, and foreign settings they get harder. Our easy to use tools can help everyone get on the same page with money.

Most budget tools assume that your income and expenses are all in the same currency, but if you live in Europe but are renting a house in the USA, or are working abroad but being paid in dollars they will fail you.

Global budget has been built by long term expats who understand what it is to live around the world. We know that life is complex and that an exchange rate moving can cause your budget to go out of whack (or suddenly have a lot of wiggle room). We make tools to match.
